NemanAirlines

Neman Airlanies is the state-owned flag carrier airline of Belarus. Headquartered in the Trinity Grodno, the airline operates a hub-and-spoke network, flying to over 150 international destinations across Africa, Asia, Europe, the Americas, and Oceania, using a fleet of more than 50 aircraft. Neman Airlanies employs more than 2,000 people. The carrier has been a member of the Oneworld alliance since October 2013, the first Belarus carrier to sign with one of the three major airline alliances.
